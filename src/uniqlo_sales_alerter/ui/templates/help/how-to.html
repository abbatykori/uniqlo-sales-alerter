Paste an invoice to extract sizes

Go to /filters/paste, paste your Uniqlo order email body, click "Detect sizes". The chip rows show clothing / pants / shoes / kids categorisation.

Snooze a filter

From /filters, click "Snooze" on a row and pick 1d / 7d / 30d / forever. Use "Resume now" to clear the snooze.

Snooze from a notification

Apprise emails ship signed action chips. Clicking "Snooze 7d" applies the same effect as the UI button, even days later (URLs expire after 30 days).
